Milk

Milk is a staple dairy product available in various forms such as whole milk, skimmed milk, and semi-skimmed milk. It's a rich source of calcium, protein, and essential vitamins like vitamin D.

Cheese

Cheese comes in numerous varieties, each with its own taste, texture, and aroma. Common types include cheddar, mozzarella, Swiss, and brie. Cheese is a versatile ingredient used in sandwiches, salads, pasta dishes, and more.

Yogurt

Yogurt is a fermented dairy product known for its creamy texture and tangy flavor. It contains probiotics, which are beneficial bacteria that promote gut health. Yogurt can be enjoyed plain or flavored and is often used in breakfast dishes, smoothies, and desserts.

Butter

Butter is a dairy product made from churned cream, typically from cow's milk. It's widely used in cooking and baking to add flavor, richness, and moisture to dishes. Butter comes in salted and unsalted varieties.
